Here are a few examples of ColdFusion websites that infoWEB have designed.

Classic Barber

The strong corporate colours of this mens' barbering company lends itself to the web. The simple design downloads quickly to give the information requested by the visitor. Currently a static information website, development is currently underway to provide an online appointments service. A first for the industry?

Hothouse Biz

A brouchureware website for a firm of management consultants. Once again, a simple but powerful layout conveys the message. The the pale grey background photographs contrast well with the strong corporate orange colour.

  

SAS Clinic

Again grey is the colour, but this time the corporate font is the main design link. The distinctive Rodchenko font links the left hand menu items with the section heading in the body of the web page.

 

James Stewart Printers

This printing firm wanted a sharp animated website, so hence the leaping frog and spinning logo. They were willing to accept the longer download time to get the movement on the site they wanted. Also the majority of their clients are large corporates that are more likely to have a fast connection to the internet.
