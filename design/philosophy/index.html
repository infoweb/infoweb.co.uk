Here at infoWEB, design is of the highest importants. We don't go in for flashy graphics with fireworks popping off and mindless sounds beating in the background. Our philosophy is simple, clean, sharp design that relies on effective layout and use of clear typefaces and appropriate colours. The websites we design download quickly because they don't rely on large graphic files.

We subscribe to the Jakob Nielsen school of web design. The design shouldn't cloud the message.
